Don't Wait Too Long

Signs You Need Air Duct Cleaning

Catching these early usually means a smaller job — and a smaller bill.

🌫️

Visible Dust Buildup

Dust collecting around vents, or a thin film resettling on furniture days after cleaning, usually means it's coming from inside the ducts.

🤧

Allergy Symptoms Indoors

If allergies flare up worse at home than outside, your ductwork may be circulating dust, pet dander, or mold spores.

💨

Weak or Uneven Airflow

Some rooms barely get airflow while others blast air — blocked or dirty ducts are a common cause.
