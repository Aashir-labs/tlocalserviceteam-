Don't Wait Too Long

Signs You Need Roofing Services

Catching these early usually means a smaller job — and a smaller bill.

💧

Water Stains on Ceiling

Usually means an active leak. It needs attention before it spreads to insulation and drywall.

🍂

Missing or Curling Shingles

A sign of age or storm damage, and a weak point where leaks tend to start.

🌪️

Recent Storm or Hail

Even minor-looking damage after a storm can lead to leaks down the line — worth a free inspection.
