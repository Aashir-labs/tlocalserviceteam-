Don't Wait Too Long

Signs You Need Garage Door Repair

Catching these early usually means a smaller job — and a smaller bill.

🔇

Loud Grinding or Banging

Unusual noise when the door opens or closes is often worn rollers or a spring starting to fail.

🚫

Door Won't Open or Close

Usually a broken spring or opener failure — and the biggest safety hazard on this list. Stop using it and call.

⚖️

Door Looks Crooked or Uneven

A door that hangs unevenly is a sign of cable or track misalignment, which can damage the door further over time.
